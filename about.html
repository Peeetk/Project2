Few words about my knowledge and skills as a bartender!

I have been making coffee since I was 13 year old!

When I was a kid, I spent my time with my father who had a little coffe shop in the town. I loved the smell of the coffe and always wanted to try it, but I was too little, so my father showed me first how to brew a real esspresso... Few weeks later he has teached me for basic. When I was 17 years old I was working by my self in the shop after school and very soon I'have took over the place. I am still running this coffe shop after 10 years. I am 27 now and I went to many schools to learn the best practices to make the best coffe, latte, macchiato, and I have gained my own coffes too that you can find in the "Menu" option. I want you to try all of them, and don't forget to leave feedback in the "Contact" menu option.
